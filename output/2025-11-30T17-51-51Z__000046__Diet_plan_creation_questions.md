Given you’re vegetarian and want brain/hormone support, we can **consider an algal DHA/EPA supplement** (completely non-fish), something like **250–500 mg DHA/EPA combined per day** if budget allows. This can support cardiovascular health, mood, and possibly attention.

### 4.2 Turmeric/curcumin

- **Target:** something like **1–3 g turmeric powder/day** (¼–½ tsp twice a day), ideally cooked in fat with black pepper.
- Role: gentle systemic anti-inflammatory, possibly supportive for joints, gut, and overall oxidative stress.

### 4.3 Ginger

- Regular fresh ginger (a few grams/day) in tea and cooking:
  - Helps digestion (especially with lentils/beans),
  - Gives mild anti-inflammatory and anti-nausea benefit.

### 4.4 Garlic & onions

- Aim for **1–2 cloves garlic/day** plus regular onion.
- Benefits: better lipid profile, improved nitric oxide (blood flow), antioxidant effect.

### 4.5 Cinnamon

- Up to **~1 tsp/day** (preferably Ceylon if used daily).
- Insulin-sensitising and nice with dates or oats.

### 4.6 Probiotics & prebiotics

We want daily gut support because it strongly interacts with mood, immunity, and skin.

- **Probiotic goal:** curd/yogurt or buttermilk most days; traditional ferments (idli/dosa batter, pickles) regularly.
- **Prebiotic goal:** plenty of onion, garlic, bananas, oats, cooled rice/potato, lentils, millets, vegetables.

### 4.7 Adaptogens (optional, with caution)

Because of nicotine withdrawal and focus issues, it’s tempting to throw in adaptogens; we’ll be conservative.

- **Ashwagandha**: may improve stress, sleep, and testosterone, but can stimulate the thyroid in some people. With your **TSH already mildly high**, I’d only use it after you’ve discussed with a local doctor and if thyroid is monitored.
- Gentler options you might consider later: **tulsi (holy basil) tea**, **brahmi (bacopa)** in low doses (can help cognition but sometimes causes GI issues).

For now, I’d treat adaptogens as **second-line extras** after sleep, protein, vitamins, and nicotine taper are better controlled.

## 5. Fasting Day Targets (Water Fast Version)

Given your nicotine withdrawal and low testosterone, if we later feel that two hard water fasts weekly is too stressful (sleep, mood, libido crash, extreme fatigue), we might shift one of them to a **very low-calorie “clean” day** instead of zero calories.
