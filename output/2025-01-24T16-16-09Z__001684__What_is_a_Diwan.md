Yes, a **diwan** is a piece of furniture commonly found in South Asian homes, including India. It is essentially a low-lying daybed or a sofa-like seating arrangement, often without a backrest or with a minimal one. Diwans are versatile and can be used for sitting during the day and sometimes for sleeping at night.

### Characteristics of a Diwan:
- **Frame:** Typically made of wood or metal.
- **Cushions/Seating:** A mattress-like cushion placed on top for comfort, often adorned with colorful covers and throw pillows.
- **Storage (optional):** Some diwans have storage space underneath, accessible through drawers or by lifting the seat.
- **Placement:** Often placed against a wall, in living rooms, verandas, or even bedrooms.
- **Style:** It can have a traditional or modern design, depending on the decor.

Diwans are both functional and decorative, making them a staple in many homes. They're perfect for lounging, hosting guests, or even serving as an extra bed.
