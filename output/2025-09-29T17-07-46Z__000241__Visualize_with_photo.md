## **Elevated Chole Tikki – A Modern Plate**

### **Core Concept**
- Keep the rustic base: spiced potato tikki with chole.

- Reimagine garnishes: microgreens, edible flowers, artisanal chutneys, textural contrasts.
- Plate with intention: layered, but minimalist.

### **Components**

1. **Tikki (Base)**
   - **Upgrade**: Make smaller, perfectly round patties of Yukon Gold potatoes mixed with a hint of smoked paprika, green chili, ginger, and a whisper of truffle oil. Pan-sear in ghee until golden with crisp edges.
   - **Twist**: For texture, fold in a little quinoa or amaranth seeds.

2. **Chole (Chickpeas)**
   - **Upgrade**: Cook chickpeas until velvety soft. Temper with roasted cumin, Kashmiri chili, black cardamom, and a drizzle of cold-pressed mustard oil at the end.
   - Blend half the chickpeas into a smooth base and fold the rest back for a silken yet chunky texture.
   - Finish with a touch of pomegranate molasses for a subtle sweet-sour lift.

3. **Chutneys (Accents)**
   - **Mint-Coriander Chutney**: Smooth, strained, almost sauce-like.
   - **Tamarind Reduction**: Slow-reduced until glossy, syrupy, and streak-worthy on the plate.
   - **Garlic Foam (optional)**: Aerated garlic-yogurt with a touch of smoked salt for modern flair.

4. **Garnishes (Luxury Touch)**
   - Microgreens (pea shoots or radish microgreens).
   - Edible flowers (nasturtium, marigold petals).
   - Pomegranate arils for color and crunch.
   - Sev replaced with **crispy lotus stem chips** or dehydrated beet crisps for refinement.

### **Plating Ideas**
- **Minimalist Modern**:
  Place two small golden tikkis slightly overlapping. Spoon the chole in a quenelle (oval) next to them. Swipe tamarind reduction across the plate. Dot mint chutney artfully. Garnish with microgreens + lotus stem chips leaning upright for height.

- **Deconstructed Street Style**:
  Serve tikkis in the center, surround with circular rings of chole, mint chutney, tamarind glaze. Scatter pomegranate seeds + edible flowers, drizzle garlic foam lightly on top.

- **Luxury Bowl**:
