   - **What to do**:

   - **What happens**: If the water is too cold or too hot, it can irritate the delicate nasal lining and nerves, causing sharp pain.

   - **What happens**: If the saline solution is too concentrated or too diluted, it can irritate or sting the nasal tissues.
