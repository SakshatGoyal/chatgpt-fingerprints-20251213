## 5️⃣ Summary & How I’d Use This Shortlist

If I were planning for you from **My Home Bhooja**, thinking both **health + cultural depth + ambience**, I’d treat them like this:

- **Closest, easiest “default” elevated options (weeknight or last-minute):**  
  - **Gudgudee (Madhapur)** – when you want pure-veg fine dining with lots of non-Indian choice but still good veg balance.  
  - **Ishtaa (Hitech City)** – very short drive, ideal for “good veg comfort food in a nice setting”.

- **When you specifically want *traditional Indian, regionally rooted* meals:**  
  - **Itlu (Jubilee Hills)** – for a properly Telugu, thali-style, culture-forward meal (and great interiors).  
  - **Utishta (Film Nagar)** – for Jain / no-onion-no-garlic meals and homely, lighter Indian plates in a boutique-like setting.

- **When you want something that feels almost “wellness retreat meets restaurant”:**  
  - **Sattvam (Banjara Hills)** – for a sattvic buffet evening; probably your best bet when you want food to feel spiritually as well as physically “clean”.

- **For a dress-up, special-occasion, multi-course dinner:**  
  - **Tatva (Road 36)** – for fine-dining plating, global vegetarian and a high-end room without compromising your vegetarian brief.
