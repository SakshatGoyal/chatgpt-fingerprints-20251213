## Will these ingredients interfere with autophagy?
- **Mineral water + CO₂:** No calories, no macronutrients; does not activate mTOR or insulin—compatible with fasting and autophagy.
- **Citric acid:** Non-caloric acidulant; no evidence it raises insulin or independently shuts off autophagy at typical beverage amounts. (Autophagy is mainly suppressed by **amino acids** and **insulin** after feeding.)
- **Natural grapefruit flavor (tiny amounts of volatile oils/extracts):** Non-caloric; unlikely to matter metabolically. Theoretical concern is **cephalic-phase insulin release (CPIR)** from taste alone—but CPIR evidence in humans is mixed and is most consistently seen with **sweet taste** (sucrose/saccharin), not sour/citrus flavor. This product contains **no sweeteners**.
- **Bottom line on autophagy control:** Fasting-induced autophagy is primarily turned **off** by caloric intake (especially protein/amino acids) and insulin signaling via **mTORC1**; a zero-calorie, unsweetened flavored mineral water is **very unlikely** to materially interfere.
