### **1. Standard Depth Sofas**

- **Depth**: 28–36 inches or more.
- **Comfort Level**:

  - Allows for layered cushions and cozy styling with throws.
- **Pros**:

---

- **Depth**: 36–48 inches.
- **Comfort Level**:
  - Built for lounging, cuddling, or shared relaxation.

- **Pros**:
