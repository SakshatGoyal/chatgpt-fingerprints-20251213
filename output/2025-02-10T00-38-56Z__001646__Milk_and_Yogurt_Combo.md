1. **Lactose Sensitivity** – If you're lactose intolerant, consuming both together might increase discomfort, bloating, or digestive issues.

2. **Digestibility** – Some people find that mixing dairy products with different fermentation levels (like milk and yogurt) can slow digestion or cause mild stomach discomfort.

3. **Probiotic Effect** – If you're eating yogurt for its probiotic benefits, excessive consumption of milk at the same time might dilute the stomach acid, potentially reducing probiotic effectiveness. This is generally not a major concern, though.
