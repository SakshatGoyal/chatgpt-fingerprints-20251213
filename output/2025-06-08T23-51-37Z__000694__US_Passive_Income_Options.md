## How to Choose & Scale

1. **Start with the “Sleep Well Stack.”**  Allocate at least one-third to the T-Bill ladder or HYSA for liquidity and psychological safety (Collins/Housel).
2. **Add measured upside.**  Cap any single higher-risk play (JEPI, Groundfloor) at a dollar amount you can emotionally afford to see fluctuate—Pant’s opportunity-cost lens.
3. **Automate contributions.**  Set up recurring transfers so the system runs without your attention (Ramit Sethi).
4. **Review annually.**  Evaluate cash-flow, re-balance, and decide whether to scale the winners into longer-term vehicles (e.g., roll T-Bills into 2-year notes, add more to Fundrise Core).

By layering safe yield first and selectively adding higher-income sleeves, you build a **margin-of-safety core with optionality for upside**—letting compounding, not constant tinkering, do the heavy lifting.
