## Notes on ingredient realism

* Natural oud, ambergris, or oakmoss are heavily regulated or cost-prohibitive in soap; artisans substitute **synthetic oudhole, ambroxan, or evernyl** respectively.  
* High-volatility citrus (Cedrat Boise) is often bolstered with **acetaldehyde and ionone aromachems** to survive saponification.  
* If you demand 100 % natural palettes, Tom Ford’s bar (uses real sandalwood) and Casa’s organic Cedrat bar come closest; all others rely on mixed naturals & synthetics for stability.
