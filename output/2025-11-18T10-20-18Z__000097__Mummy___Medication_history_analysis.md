**Labs that help:**

- **Thyroid function tests**

- **Electrolytes**

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Questions About Metabolism & ‚ÄúClozapine Readiness‚Äù

If you ever move toward **clozapine** or continue/use **olanzapine long term**, you must know her **metabolic baseline**.

### Q5. How much have the medicines affected her sugar, cholesterol, and weight-related risk?

**Why:**
Olanzapine and especially clozapine strongly affect **weight, blood sugar, and lipids**.

**Labs:**

  - Total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, triglycerides

### Q6. Is her liver and kidney function good enough for stronger medicines (like clozapine) and for handling multiple drugs?

**Why:**
Almost all psych meds are processed by the **liver** and/or **kidneys**. Clozapine especially needs a reasonably healthy baseline.

**Labs:**

- **Liver function tests (LFT)**
  - AST, ALT, ALP, GGT, bilirubin, albumin
- **Renal function tests (RFT)**
  - Urea, creatinine, eGFR
- **Serum electrolytes** (if not already done above)
