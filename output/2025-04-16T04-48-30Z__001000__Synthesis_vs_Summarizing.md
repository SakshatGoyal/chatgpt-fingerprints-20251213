### üß† What is *synthesis*?

**Synthesis** is the process of combining multiple distinct elements ‚Äî ideas, perspectives, findings, or pieces of information ‚Äî into a new, cohesive understanding or framework. It creates something **greater than the sum of its parts**.

### üÜö How is synthesis different from summarizing?

|                 | **Summarizing**                          | **Synthesis**                                      |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| **Goal**         | Condense information                     | Combine to generate new understanding              |

- Weaving together different strands of thought into a single thread.
